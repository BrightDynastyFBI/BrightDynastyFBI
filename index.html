About the Division

The Bright Dynasty FBI Division is the investigative and enforcement branch of the Bright Dynasty community. Agents monitor for rule violations, harassment, cheating, and impersonation, and respond to reports filed by any member.

  • Investigate and resolve community reports fairly and quickly.
  • Track known suspects and repeat offenders.
  • Support staff with evidence review and case documentation.
  • Keep the community a safe, trustworthy place to play.
